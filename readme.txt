== Description ==
This is a plugin allowing you to embed a slideshow directly into your sidebar or post/page, no limiting your number of pictures and number of slideshows.

Transition and waiting effect using Flash technology, and more many other effects that suit your flavor.
You can also include text in each picture.
For its friendly user’s interface, you can easily create galleries, manage pictures, and choose how your pictures will be displayed.
To see its demo, please click here: http://axcoto.com/vincent/article/99

How to use:
Go to Ax-Slideshow on left sidebar in admin 
Creat gallery on that page

== Installation ==

1/ Please download here: http://wordpress.org/extend/plugins/axcoto-slideshow-plugin/

2/ Extract and upload to your Plugin directory (wp-content/plugins/)

3/ Go to the 'Plugins' Page of your Administration Area and activate the Plugin. You’ll see Ax-Slideshow on your sidebar where you can manage your galleries and slideshows
